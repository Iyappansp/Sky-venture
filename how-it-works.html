Your Flight Experience Explained

Four simple steps to earning your wings

1

Arrival & Check-In

Arrive at our facility about an hour before your flight time. Our friendly team will welcome you, check you in, and help you complete the necessary waivers. You'll get to see the wind tunnel in action and feel the excitement building up!

  • Free parking available
  • Lockers for personal belongings
2

Training Session

You'll join your certified flight instructor for a fun and informative training session. They'll teach you proper body positioning, hand signals for communication inside the tunnel, and safety procedures. No experience needed!

  • 20-minute classroom session
  • Learn the "neutral" body position
3

Gear Up

Look the part! We provide all the specialized gear you need, including a flight suit, helmet, goggles, and earplugs. Our instructors will help you get suited up and ensure everything fits perfectly for a safe flight.

  • Professional grade equipment
  • Glasses-friendly goggles available
4

  • 1-on-1 instructor guidance
  • Flight certificate included
