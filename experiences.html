What to Expect

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure. Here's how your flight experience will unfold:

1. Check-In

Arrive 45 minutes early to check in and complete waivers.

2. Training

Join a class for a comprehensive safety briefing and flight instruction.

3. Gear Up

Suit up in your flight gear, helmet, and goggles.

4. Fly!

Enter the tunnel and experience the thrill of freefall with your instructor.
