What is Indoor Skydiving?

Experience the sensation of freefall without jumping from a plane

Indoor skydiving uses a vertical wind tunnel to generate a powerful, precisely controlled column of air that simulates the true freefall experience of skydiving—without the need to jump from an aircraft. This innovative setup recreates the sensation of floating and maneuvering in mid-air, allowing participants to safely experience the thrill of flight in a fully supervised environment.
